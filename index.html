OUR STORY

Established in 1989, PT. TASINDO TASSA INDUSTRIES is one of the leading Manufacturer of soft bags in Indonesia. We hold very strong global export - oriented market in USA, Japan, Germany, Belgium and most major European Countries.

Currently, the Company succesfully achieves a monthly production of around 200.000 units of bags. As the company has invested in its extensive-range of manufacturing facilities such as welding, cutting, slitting and sewing machines.”.
